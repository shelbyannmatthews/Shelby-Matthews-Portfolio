Llama Jump

Game Design, Unity & C#

Llama Jump is a platform game, requiring the player (farmer) to navigate its world to retrieve a runaway llama. The player must use WASD or
the arrow keys to move and use the space bar to jump. In this project, I have created the sprites using Piskel.

Click one of the images to play!
